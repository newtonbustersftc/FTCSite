Newton Busters FTC is sponsored by Girl Scouts

The Newton Busters FTC team has a sponsorship from Girl Scouts since the team is focused on women empowerment, trying to improve meager statistics that there are only 25% women in STEM occupations. There are far fewer girls participating in robotics clubs than boys. However our teams is not limited to the girls.
